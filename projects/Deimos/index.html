Deimos is a launcher for games by King Jellycycle!

Being developed using pywebview, Deimos is a launcher that will contain most games developed by Jelly!
Featuring:
  • Easy download and installation of games
  • Patching for each game
  • News and information from my blog and individual game updates!

Images! (WIP)


Why another launcher? Why not Steam or Epic?

To put it simply, control and easy access. Control over the presentation of my projects, blog posts and anything else that may be important!

A central location where you, the user, can easily access most content of mine!
